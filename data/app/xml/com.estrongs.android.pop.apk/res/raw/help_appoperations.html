Manage applications

Click App Manager icon (the second icon from right to left), will list all apps installed in the system.

Long press apps icon you wanna uninstall or backup, operation dialog pops-up which includes the options of Uninstall and Backup.

You can choose one to uninstall or backup, Or multi-operate.

You can also put a shortcut of the app on the Desktop.

Default backup directory is /sdcard/backups/apps. To change the directory, click MENU/setting，modify the options. Home
