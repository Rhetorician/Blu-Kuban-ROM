Search files

Click MENU/new/search, Or you can click the Search icon on the toolbar directly.

Select the option of Customize Types, input the keywords what you want to search(such as love.mp3, aaa, txt, etc.).

Directly to find all default types of video and audio files, You can also click OK button.

To cancel the search task, drag down the status bar, Click the task progress bar and cancel it.
