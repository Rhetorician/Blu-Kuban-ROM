Manage task

Click MENU/Managers/Task Manager, Or click task manager icon (slide mangers) on the toolbar.

To kill tasks in the Application tab, click one or more tasks (click again to unselect it) , and then click the Kill button.

To view Services, click the Services tab, you can see the running services. The services cannot be killed because they are system inbuilt and will restart each time you kill them, and killing them will cause the unstablity of the system.

To view CPU/Memory Info, click MENU/Settings in task manager,check the options.

WARNING: the Info is not available in some systems. If you meet problems, please uncheck this option.

To sort by CPU/Memory, click sort button, choose one of the options of CPU/Memory Ascending and Descending.

To switch to some application (including EStrongs File Explorer) , long press it,and choose Switch To.

To set a notification icon (it's only a shortcut) on the left corner of the screen, click MENU/Setting and find the option.
