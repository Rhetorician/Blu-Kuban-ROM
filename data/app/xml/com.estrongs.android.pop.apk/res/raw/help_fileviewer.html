View Files

View document

Click and open this file, you can open files of different types if you've installed the right app to open them.

View by Mode

Click the Grid icon (the rightmost) on the toolbar, you can view files and folders in icon or list mode.

Click MENU/Setting/view, and click Change View,choose one of three options of icon, list and detail.

View by Size

Click MENU/Setting/view, click Size and choose one of the options of Normal and Small.

Sort Files

Click MENU/Setting/view, Click Sort by and choose one of the options of Name, Type, Size, Modified and Random.

Open As

You can open a file as TEXT, IMAGE, etc. from long press a file and choose Open As. If the file isn't of the type you choose, you will see unexpected file content.

Play Video/Audio Files

Click and open this file, now it supports MP3, AMR, WAV, WMA, MP4, 3GP, WMV format in sdcard and remote PC. You can try different media players.

View Pictures

Click and open picture, currently it supports jpg, png, gif, bmp in sdcard and remote PC.

You can set pictures as wallpaper and contact icon, view or set the detail properties of picture.

Show Hidden Files

To hide files, add . in front of file name, and then click MENU/Setting, uncheck the option of Show Hidden Files.
